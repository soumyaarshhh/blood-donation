About LifeDrop

We Connect Donors
With Those in Need

LifeDrop is a voluntary blood donation initiative dedicated to building a self-sufficient blood supply across the country. We make donating as easy as possible — find a camp, register, and save a life.

Every 2 seconds someone needs blood. Surgeries, cancer treatments, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries all rely on donated blood. There is no substitute — only human donors can help.

Am I Eligible?

Donation Eligibility Criteria

You CAN donate if:
  • You are 18–65 years old
  • You weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • You are in good general health
  • Your hemoglobin level is ≥ 12.5 g/dL
  • It's been 56+ days since your last donation
You CANNOT donate if:
  • You have a cold, flu, or fever today
  • You are pregnant or recently gave birth
  • You've had a tattoo/piercing in last 6 months
  • You are on certain medications (ask staff)
  • You have traveled to malaria-risk areas recently

Whole blood donors can donate every 56 days (about every 2 months). Platelet donors can donate more frequently — up to 24 times per year.
